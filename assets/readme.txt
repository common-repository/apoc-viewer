APOC (A Piece of Content) enables people to create interactive XR content on their own without a single line of coding on a web-based platform and you can share and monetize their content within the platform.

== Description ==
Our mission is to empower people to create and experience interactive XR content easily on a web-based,
zero-coding platform without device and infrastructure restrictions.
The most intuitive a-few-clicks-away UI, drag-and-drop features,
and real-time collaboration are eligible for teachers and students to create
and run their own content with high engagement, achievement, and creativity in various education.

== Installation ==
1. Go to `Plugins` in the Admin menu
2. Click on the button `Add new`
3. Search for `APOC` and click 'Install Now' or click on the `upload` link to upload `apoc.zip`
4. Click on `Activate plugin`

== Screenshots ==

1. Click the Add New Post! button on the APOC plug-in main!
2. Please open Blocks Tab and select APOC Block!
3. Visit https://apoc.day and click the Share Content button to copy url!
4. Paste the copied url into the created APOC block!
5. You can embed content created by APOC!
